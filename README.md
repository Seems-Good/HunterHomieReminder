# HunterHomieReminder
Simple WoW addon to track and display when your Hunter pet is not around.

<img width="387" height="180" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/92a52d03-5dc5-4b29-8014-8bde70524ef6" />

**Setup:**
- Frame default is middle of screen.
- Dismiss your pet and right click the frame to unlock. Move and Resize the frame to your liking.

**Features:**
- No complex state stacking, if we have **no** pet (dead of dismissed) we show the frame.
- Minimal addon footprint respecting player in-game performance.
